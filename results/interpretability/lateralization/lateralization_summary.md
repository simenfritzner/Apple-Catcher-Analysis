# Lateralization Analysis Summary

## Research Question
Does the EEGNet model use contralateral sensorimotor patterns
(the hallmark of genuine motor imagery classification)?

## Key Results

### MI_left
- Left hemisphere saliency:  0.001264
- Right hemisphere saliency: 0.001617
- Post-cue laterality index: 0.0819
- Expected for genuine MI: right hemisphere > left hemisphere
- Observed: right > left

### MI_right
- Left hemisphere saliency:  0.001034
- Right hemisphere saliency: 0.001454
- Post-cue laterality index: -0.1520
- Expected for genuine MI: left hemisphere > right hemisphere
- Observed: right > left

## Verdict
MI_left post-cue LI: 0.0819
MI_right post-cue LI: -0.1520
**NO contralateral lateralization — NOT genuine MI patterns**
